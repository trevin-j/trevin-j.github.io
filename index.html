About Me

My name is Trevin Jones. I'm a CS student that loves leaning and experimenting with new things. This website is a place where you can learn a little bit about me and where I can show off some of my projects.

My whole life I have been fascinated with technology. My first experience with programming was with TI-Basic on my calculator as a freshman in high school. When I was a senior in high school, I finally began learning how to program with Python. Since then, I have spent time learning many other languages and technologies. I have several personal projects that I work on, and many of my projects can be found on my GitHub page.
