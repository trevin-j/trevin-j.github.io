About Me

I grew up in Montana, and I have alwyas been fascinated with technology. I'm currently a student at Brigham Young University-Idaho studying Computer Science. I frequently consider myself self-taught in programming, but my courses at BYU-I have been so helpful in guiding my learning.

My first experience with programming is working with TI-Basic on my TI-84 Plus CE as a freshman in high school. When I was a senior in high school, I finally began learning how to program with Python. Since then, I have spent time learning numerous other languages and technologies.

I love to develop software in my free time. I have several personal projects that I am always working on, and many of my projects can be found on my GitHub page. My first programming language was Python, but recently I have been enjoying working with several different languages. Right now C++ has to be my favorite, due to the unique challenges it throws at me.
